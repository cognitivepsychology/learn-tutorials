Step 1: Go to plot.ly

Fill in your address, desired username, and password, and click the “Sign up” button. Or sign up using Facebook, Google+, Twitter or Github.

You’ll get an email from accounts@plot.ly to verify your address after you’ve signed up.

Step 2: Get started!

Visit plot.ly/plot to start making your own plots

Visit plot.ly/learn to check out tutorials on how to use the GUI and the APIs

Visit plot.ly/feed to view some of the best plots out there!
